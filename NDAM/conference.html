THE CONFERENCE
This conference is part of a series that brings together a selected group of microeconomics scholars to explore new research directions at the nexus of theory and empirics.

The conference will span three days, November 12 to 14, 2015. There will be around twenty 25-minute talks. The venue is Caltech Institute of Technology in Pasadena, CA. The conference is open to invitees only, including several PhD students. The conference will start with a cocktail reception on November 12 and will end in the late afternoon of November 14. We will offer lunch and light snacks every day, as well as a conference dinner on November 13.

All travel and hotel expenses are covered by the participants.

This conference is supported by the Caltech Division of Humanities and Social Sciences.
